Tutoring

Sometimes the best way to face and sort out life's difficulties is to take a break and read some metaphysics.

I offer one-on-one philosophical tutoring. You can take with me courses on great philosophical figures such as Wittgenstein, or on specialized topics (e.g., Good Life), where we cover different periods and philosophical approaches to the questions we discuss. Furthermore, reading together philosophical texts of your choice is a possibility.
